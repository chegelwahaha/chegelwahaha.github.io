课程理念 | Educational Philosophy

本课程以“计算思维”为核心，在6年级课程的基础上，进一步培养学生运用信息技术解决问题的能力，并开始接触代码编程和人工智能。课程强调进阶性、综合性、探索性、前沿性和趣味性。

Building upon the foundation of the 6th grade curriculum, this course continues to cultivate students' ability to solve problems using information technology, with a focus on "Computational Thinking." It introduces code-based programming and artificial intelligence, emphasizing progression, integration, exploration, cutting-edge concepts, and engagement.

指导原则 | Guiding Principles

本课程遵循中国教育部《中小学信息技术课程标准》、美国CSTA K-12计算机科学标准以及ISTE学生标准。

This course adheres to the guidelines of the Chinese Ministry of Education's "Information Technology Curriculum Standards for Primary and Secondary Schools," the American CSTA K-12 Computer Science Standards, and the ISTE Standards for Students.

课程设置设计的思想 | Course Design Philosophy

本课程采用“项目式学习”（Project-Based Learning）、“探究式学习”（Inquiry-Based Learning）和“翻转课堂”（Flipped Classroom）的设计思想。

This course incorporates the design philosophies of "Project-Based Learning," "Inquiry-Based Learning," and "Flipped Classroom."

课程内容 | Course Content

本课程主要包含以下内容：

  • Micro:bits回顾与游戏设计 (Micro:bits Review and Game Design)
  • Microsoft Office回顾与进阶 (Microsoft Office Review and Advanced Features)
  • Python Turtle编程 (Python Turtle Programming): 学习使用Python Turtle进行图形化编程，掌握变量、循环、条件判断、函数等编程概念。(Learn graphical programming using Python Turtle, mastering programming concepts such as variables, loops, conditional statements, and functions.)
  • 3D建模 (3D Modeling): 进阶3D建模。(Advanced 3D modeling.)
  • 二进制 (Binary Numbers): 学习二进制的概念及与十进制的转换。(Learn the concept of binary numbers and their conversion to and from decimal numbers.)
  • Python Turtle & AI: 结合Python Turtle编程与人工智能初步知识。(Combine Python Turtle programming with introductory knowledge of Artificial Intelligence.)

评估方式 | Assessment Methods

本课程采用多元化的评估方式，包括课堂观察、作品评价、互评与自评、随堂测验、项目报告/展示和代码审查。

This course uses a variety of assessment methods, including classroom observation, project evaluation, peer and self-assessment, quizzes, project reports/presentations, and code review.
