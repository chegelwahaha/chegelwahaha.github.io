6年级IT课程介绍 | Grade 6 IT Course Introduction

3DPrinting Modelling 3D建模

Micro:bits

课程理念 | Educational Philosophy

本课程以“计算思维”为核心，培养学生利用信息技术解决问题的能力。课程强调兴趣驱动、实践导向、创新培养、合作学习和个性发展。

This course focuses on "Computational Thinking" and aims to cultivate students' ability to solve problems using information technology. The curriculum emphasizes interest-driven learning, practical application, fostering innovation, collaborative learning, and personalized development.

指导原则 | Guiding Principles

本课程遵循中国教育部《中小学信息技术课程标准》、美国CSTA K-12计算机科学标准以及ISTE学生标准。

This course follows the guidelines of the Chinese Ministry of Education's "Information Technology Curriculum Standards for Primary and Secondary Schools," the American CSTA K-12 Computer Science Standards, and the ISTE Standards for Students.

课程设置设计的思想 | Course Design Philosophy

本课程采用“项目式学习”（Project-Based Learning）和“游戏化学习”（Gamification）的设计思想，设置基础任务和挑战任务，满足不同层次学生的需求。

This course adopts the design philosophies of "Project-Based Learning" and "Gamification," incorporating both basic and challenging tasks to cater to students of different levels.

课程内容 | Course Content

本课程主要包含以下内容：

  • 十指输入法和练习打字 (Ten-finger Typing and Typing Practice)
  • Microsoft Office操作 (Microsoft Office Operations)
  • Micro:bits编程 (Micro:bits Programming)
  • 3D建模 (3D Modeling)
  • AI概念和机器学习 (AI Concepts and Machine Learning)
  • mBots2学习 (mBots2 Learning)
  • 游戏编程 (Game Programming)

评估方式 | Assessment Methods

本课程采用多元化的评估方式，包括课堂观察、作品评价、互评与自评、随堂测验和项目报告/展示。

This course adopts a variety of assessment methods, including classroom observation, project evaluation, peer and self-assessment, quizzes, and project reports/presentations.
