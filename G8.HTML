课程理念 | Educational Philosophy

本课程以“项目驱动、问题导向”为核心，强调计算思维的培养和信息技术的综合应用，进一步深化学生的编程能力，并引导学生利用信息技术解决跨学科问题。注重项目驱动、问题导向、团队合作、技术进阶、跨学科融合和成果导向。

This course centers around "project-driven, problem-oriented" learning, emphasizing the cultivation of computational thinking and the comprehensive application of information technology. It further deepens students' programming abilities and guides them to use information technology to solve interdisciplinary problems. The course focuses on project-based learning, problem-solving, teamwork, technical advancement, interdisciplinary integration, and outcome-based assessment.

指导原则 | Guiding Principles

本课程遵循中国教育部《义务教育信息科技课程标准（2022年版）》8年级的主要内容、美国CSTA K-12计算机科学标准以及ISTE学生标准。

This course follows the main content of the 8th grade of the "Compulsory Education Information Technology Curriculum Standards (2022 Edition)" of the Chinese Ministry of Education, the American CSTA K-12 Computer Science Standards, and the ISTE Standards for Students.

课程设置设计的思想 | Course Design Philosophy

本课程采用“项目式学习”（Project-Based Learning）、“协作式学习”（Collaborative Learning）和“设计思维”（Design Thinking）的设计思想。

This course adopts the design philosophies of "Project-Based Learning," "Collaborative Learning," and "Design Thinking."

课程内容 | Course Content

本课程主要包含以下内容：

  • Python回顾与游戏设计 (Python Review and Game Design)
  • Microsoft Office回顾与数据分析 (Microsoft Office Review and Data Analysis)
  • HTML网页设计与制作 (HTML Web Design and Development): 学习HTML、CSS和JavaScript基础知识，使用Adobe Dreamweaver进行网页设计与制作，并在Github Pages上搭建个人网站。(Learn the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, use Adobe Dreamweaver for web design and development, and build a personal website on Github Pages.)
  • 3D建模 (3D Modeling): 进阶3D建模。(Advanced 3D modeling.)
  • Excel统计学计算器 (Excel Statistical Calculator): 使用Excel制作统计学计算器。(Create a statistical calculator using Excel.)
  • 跨学科网站项目 (Interdisciplinary Website Project): 以团队形式，结合其他学科知识，设计和制作一个跨学科网站。(In teams, design and create an interdisciplinary website, integrating knowledge from other subjects.)
  • AI知识介绍 (Introduction to AI): 介绍AI的基础知识。(Introduce the fundamentals of AI.)

评估方式 | Assessment Methods

本课程采用多元化的评估方式，包括课堂观察、作品评价、互评与自评、随堂测验和项目报告/展示。

This course utilizes a variety of assessment methods, including classroom observation, project evaluation, peer and self-assessment, quizzes, and project reports/presentations.
